Golden ratio of Valium

Hirst’s Valium and the Golden ratio The artist Damien Hirst has a painting called Valium (2000) (£6k-8k at Christie’s) which is based on a geometrically repeating pattern coupled with randomised colors. The pattern of locations resembles that found in a sun flower, and has a beautiful connection to the golden ratio. Numberphile has a video1 that explains the maths quite nicely, let’s demostrate the idea here.
